About flaming

Disruptive Community

Flaming disrupts meaningful conversations, hindering the ability of community members to engage in productive discussions and share diverse perspectives.

Social Dynamics

Flaming can contribute to social polarization by reinforcing existing biases and divisions, making it difficult for people with different opinions to find common ground.

Emotional Impact

Individuals targeted by flaming often experience stress, anxiety, and emotional distress due to the hostile and personal nature of the attacks. Continuous exposure to flaming can erode self-esteem and confidence, especially among younger individuals and those with vulnerable self-perceptions.
